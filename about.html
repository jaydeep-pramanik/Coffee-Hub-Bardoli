Our Story

Born from a Love of Coffee

From a neighbourhood dream to Bardoli's most-loved café — rated 4.9 stars by our community.

Our Beginning

Born from a love of coffee.

Coffee Hub started with a simple belief: that Bardoli deserved a café it could truly call its own. Nestled in Sundaram Square on Lal Bahadur Shastri Road, we opened our doors to the community of Rushikesh Nagar and Radhabaug Society — and they welcomed us with open arms.

Today, with a 4.9-star Google rating and thousands of happy guests, Coffee Hub is more than a café — it's a daily ritual for the people of Bardoli. Whether you come for the coffee, the food, or simply to breathe, we promise to make every visit worth the trip.
